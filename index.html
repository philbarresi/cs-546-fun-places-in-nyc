Fun Places in NYC

Meatpacking District

The meatpacking district used to be the primary location for butchers and meat importing was processed in NYC. It had many clubs, and now it has many restaurants and lofts.

Washington Park

Washington Park has many musicians and has a fountain, and some old buildings in it. It's very close to SOHO!

Greenwhich Village

The epicenter of the city's 1960s counterculture movement, the tree-lined streets of Greenwich Village are now a hub of popular cafes, bars and restaurants. Jazz clubs and Off-Broadway Theaters can also be found amid the brownstones and New York University buildings.
